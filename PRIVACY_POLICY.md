## 1. Information We Collect

### 1.1 Photos and Images

When you use FridgeSnap to analyze your refrigerator or pantry:

- **What We Collect:** The photos you take or select are sent to our servers for AI analysis.
- **How We Use It:** Photos are processed by AI to detect ingredients and generate recipes.
- **Retention:** Photos are processed in real-time and are **NOT stored** on our servers. They are immediately discarded after processing is complete.
- **No Human Review:** Your photos are never viewed by humans. They are only processed by automated AI systems.

### 1.2 Usage Data

We automatically collect certain information when you use the App:

- **Device Information:** Device type, operating system version, unique device identifiers
- **App Usage:** Features used, time spent in app, crash reports
- **Analytics:** Anonymous usage patterns to improve the App

This data is collected through standard analytics tools and is never linked to personally identifiable information.

### 1.3 Subscription Information

If you subscribe to FridgeSnap Pro:

- **Payment Processing:** All payments are handled by Apple through the App Store. We do NOT collect or store your payment information (credit card numbers, billing address, etc.).
- **Subscription Status:** We receive confirmation of your subscription status from Apple/RevenueCat to unlock Pro features.
